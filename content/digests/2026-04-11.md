A GMC Sierra EV driver has shared their experience with a rooftop solar panel system, home backup battery, and bidirectional EV charging. The setup allows the driver to charge their EV using solar power and also send power back to the house when needed. This trifecta of home solar, battery, and EV has raised questions about the actual cost savings. According to the driver's experience, the system has provided significant savings on energy bills. The driver's GMC Sierra EV is equipped with a bidirectional charging system, which enables the vehicle to act as a backup power source for the home.
