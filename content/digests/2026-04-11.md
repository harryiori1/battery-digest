## 01 GMC Sierra EV Driver Shares Home Solar + Battery Experience
A GMC Sierra EV driver has shared their experience with a rooftop solar panel system, home backup battery, and bidirectional EV charging. The setup allows the driver to charge their EV using solar power and also send power back to the house when needed. This trifecta of home solar, battery, and EV has raised questions about the potential cost savings. According to the driver's experience, the system has provided significant savings on energy bills. The use of bidirectional EV charging has also enabled the driver to power their home during outages, making it a reliable backup system. As the adoption of EVs and home solar systems continues to grow, such setups are likely to become more common. ## 02 Ziehl-Abegg Launches SolarBoost Solar Panel System for Bus Fleets
Ziehl-Abegg has launched a new solar panel system called SolarBoost, designed specifically for bus fleets. The system aims to reduce carbon emissions and increase energy efficiency in public transportation. The SolarBoost system can be integrated into existing bus fleets, providing a sustainable and environmentally friendly solution. With the growing demand for eco-friendly transportation, Ziehl-Abegg's SolarBoost system is poised to make a significant impact in the industry. The system's specifications and pricing have not been disclosed, but it is expected to be a game-changer for bus fleets looking to reduce their carbon footprint.
