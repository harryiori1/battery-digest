## 01 BYD Upgrades Top-Selling EVs with 5-Minute Flash Charging
BYD has announced that it is upgrading some of its top-selling electric vehicles (EVs) with its new Flash Charging system, which enables charging in as little as 5 minutes. The system was first launched in luxury vehicles like the Denza Z9 GT, but will now be rolled out to other models. This move is expected to enhance the competitiveness of BYD's EVs in the market, particularly in regions where charging infrastructure is limited. The Flash Charging system is a significant improvement over traditional charging methods, which can take hours to fully charge a vehicle. With this technology, BYD aims to provide its customers with a more convenient and efficient charging experience. ## 02 Tianneng Power's Financial Report Reveals Challenges in Lithium Battery Business
Tianneng Power's financial report has revealed that its lithium battery business is not profitable, despite investing 10 billion in the sector. The company's lead-acid battery business has reached its peak, and it is now focusing on developing its lithium battery business. However, the report shows that the company is facing challenges in this area, including high production costs and intense competition. Tianneng Power's financial report is a significant indicator of the challenges faced by companies in the lithium battery industry, particularly in China. ## 03 Tesla Owner Uses Emergency Solar to Trickle Charge in Desert
A Tesla owner had to use emergency solar panels to charge their vehicle after running out of battery in the Atacama Desert. The owner was driving a Tesla Model X on a road trip across the Americas when the vehicle's battery ran out of charge. With no charging infrastructure in sight, the owner had to deploy emergency solar panels on the side of the highway to trickle charge the vehicle. The incident highlights the challenges of long-distance EV travel, particularly in regions where charging infrastructure is limited. It also demonstrates the importance of having emergency charging solutions, such as solar panels, to ensure the safety and convenience of EV owners.
