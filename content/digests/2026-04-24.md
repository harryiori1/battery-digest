## 01 BYD Upgrades Top-Selling EVs with 5-Min Flash Charging
BYD has launched its new Flash Charging system, enabling EV charging in as little as 5 minutes, in luxury vehicles like the Denza Z9 GT. The company is now set to roll out this technology to some of its best-selling EVs, significantly improving their charging times. This move is expected to boost BYD's competitiveness in the EV market, where fast charging has become a key differentiator. With this upgrade, BYD's top-selling EVs will be able to charge from 0 to 80% in just 5 minutes, making them more appealing to customers who value convenience and speed. ## 02 Big Ferries Are Becoming Battery-First Systems
Ferries are transitioning to battery-first systems, reducing fuel costs and increasing efficiency. This shift is driven by the need to reduce emissions and operating costs, as well as to improve the overall passenger experience. Battery-first systems allow ferries to run on electric power for most of their journey, with diesel engines serving as a backup. This approach not only reduces fuel consumption but also lowers emissions, making it a more sustainable option for the maritime industry. As the technology continues to evolve, we can expect to see more ferries adopting battery-first systems, leading to a significant reduction in greenhouse gas emissions. [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/04/24/big-ferries-are-becoming-battery-first-systems/)
