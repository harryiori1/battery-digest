## 01 GM Energy Home System Turns EV into Backup Battery Power
General Motors has introduced a new system that allows homeowners to use their EV as a backup power source during outages. The GM Energy Home System enables homeowners to harness the energy stored in their EV's battery to power their homes. This innovative solution provides a reliable and efficient way to keep homes powered during blackouts. The system is designed to work seamlessly with GM's EVs, providing a convenient and sustainable solution for homeowners. As the demand for energy storage and backup power solutions continues to grow, GM's new system is poised to make a significant impact in the market. ## 02 BYD Secures 100,000-Vehicle Order from Car Inc for Flash Charging EVs
BYD has secured a large order of 100,000 vehicles from Car Inc, a major player in the EV market. The order is significant, as it marks a major push for BYD's flash charging technology.
