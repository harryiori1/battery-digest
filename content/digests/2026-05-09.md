## 01 BYD to Launch 2026 Seagull with LiDAR on May 11
BYD is set to launch the 2026 Seagull on May 11, which will be the company's first entry-level EV equipped with LiDAR technology. The Seagull is expected to be a significant addition to BYD's lineup, offering advanced safety features at an affordable price point. The inclusion of LiDAR technology is a notable development, as it is typically found in higher-end vehicles. BYD's decision to include LiDAR in the Seagull suggests a commitment to making advanced safety features more accessible to a wider range of consumers. ## 02 Chinese EV Makers Deny Being Summoned Over Battery Capacity Locking
Chinese EV makers have denied being summoned by regulators over battery capacity locking, despite reports suggesting that companies had been called in for questioning. The allegations centered on the practice of locking certain battery capacities, potentially misleading consumers about the true range of their vehicles. The EV makers have denied any wrongdoing, and it remains to be seen how the situation will unfold. The incident highlights the ongoing scrutiny of the EV industry, particularly with regards to battery technology and consumer transparency. ## 03 BYD's Fleet-Focused Brand Linghui Launches e9 Flagship Sedan with Flash Charging
BYD's fleet-focused brand Linghui has launched the e9 flagship sedan, which is equipped with flash charging technology. The launch of the e9 is a notable development in the fleet EV market, where BYD is looking to establish a strong presence.
