## 01 GM Energy Home System Allows EVs to Power Homes
General Motors has introduced a new system that enables electric vehicles to provide backup power to homes during outages. The GM Energy Home System allows homeowners to use their EVs as a source of energy, providing a reliable and efficient way to keep their homes powered. This innovation has the potential to revolutionize the way we think about energy storage and backup power. With the ability to use their EVs as a backup power source, homeowners can reduce their reliance on traditional generators and enjoy a more sustainable and environmentally friendly solution. According to [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/09/gm-car-battery-backup-gm-energy-home-system-video/), the system is designed to work seamlessly with GM's EVs, providing a convenient and user-friendly experience for homeowners.

## 02 BYD Secures 100,000-Vehicle Order for Flash Charging EVs
BYD has secured a significant order for 100,000 vehicles from Car Inc, with all vehicles to be equipped with flash charging technology. This order marks a major push for BYD's flash charging technology, which enables EVs to charge quickly and efficiently. The order is a testament to the growing demand for EVs with advanced charging capabilities, and BYD's position as a leader in the EV market. According to [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/05/09/byd-secures-100000-vehicle-order-from-car-inc/), the order is valued at millions of dollars and will help to further establish BYD as a major player in the EV industry.

## 03 BYD to Launch Entry-Level EV with LiDAR on May 11
BYD is set to launch its new entry-level EV, the 2026 Seagull, on May 11. The vehicle will be equipped with LiDAR technology, making it BYD's first entry-level EV to feature this advanced safety feature. The 2026 Seagull is expected to be a significant addition to BYD's lineup, offering customers a more affordable option with advanced technology. According to [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/05/09/byd-launch-2026-seagull-may-11-lidar/), the vehicle will be priced competitively and will offer a range of features, including flash charging and advanced safety features.
