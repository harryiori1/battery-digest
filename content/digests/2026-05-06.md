## 01 CATL Signs 60GWh Sodium-Ion Battery Deal with HyperStrong
CATL, the world's largest battery maker, has signed a 60GWh sodium-ion battery deal with energy storage integrator HyperStrong. This marks the largest sodium-ion battery order ever placed and is equivalent to half of all energy storage batteries CATL delivered in 2025. The deal is a significant milestone in the commercialization of sodium-ion batteries, which are seen as a potential alternative to lithium-ion batteries. CATL has stated that it has "overcome the challenges of the entire sodium-ion battery mass production process," making sodium-ion batteries mainstream-ready. This development is expected to have a major impact on the battery industry, with sodium-ion batteries potentially offering a more cost-effective and sustainable solution for energy storage.
