## 01 California Senate Bill Could Turn Home Batteries into Moneymakers
A new bill in the California senate, SB 913, could revolutionize the way home backup batteries are used, allowing utilities and grid operators to treat them like power plants. This would enable programs that pay homeowners for sharing excess energy capacity during peak demand, potentially turning home batteries into a new source of income. The bill aims to incentivize the adoption of energy storage systems, promoting a more efficient and resilient grid. According to [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/04/25/new-bill-in-california-senate-could-turn-your-home-battery-into-a-moneymaker/), this move could have significant implications for the energy storage industry, enabling new business models and revenue streams.
