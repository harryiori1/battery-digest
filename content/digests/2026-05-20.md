## 01 Ford Launches Ford Energy Subsidiary to Build 20 GWh of Battery Storage Annually
Ford has officially unveiled Ford Energy, a wholly owned subsidiary that will manufacture and sell US-assembled battery energy storage systems (BESS) for utilities, data centers, and large industrial customers. The new unit plans to produce 20 GWh of energy storage annually from its Kentucky gigafactory. This move marks a significant expansion of Ford's presence in the energy storage market, and demonstrates the company's commitment to providing innovative solutions for customers. With a planned production capacity of 20 GWh, Ford Energy is poised to become a major player in the battery energy storage market. ## 02 The UK Delivers Europe’s Largest Vanadium Flow Battery System
Invinity Energy Systems has delivered 20.7 megawatt-hours of vanadium flow batteries to the Copwood VFB Energy Hub in East Sussex. Once the project enters service later in 2026, Invinity says it will be Europe’s largest vanadium flow battery installation. The Copwood VFB Energy Hub is a significant project that demonstrates the potential of vanadium flow batteries to provide long-duration energy storage solutions. With a capacity of 20.7 megawatt-hours, the system will be able to store excess energy generated by renewable sources and release it when needed, helping to stabilize the grid and reduce reliance on fossil fuels. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/11/uk-delivers-europes-largest-vanadium-flow-battery-system/)

## 03 BYD Upgrading Top Selling EVs with 5-Min Flash Charging and Nearly 400 Miles of Range
BYD has launched its new Flash Charging system, enabling EV charging in as little as 5 minutes, in luxury vehicles like the Denza Z9 GT. Now, it’s rolling out to some of its best-selling vehicles. The Flash Charging system is a significant upgrade that will provide BYD customers with faster and more convenient charging options. With the ability to charge to nearly 400 miles of range in just 5 minutes, BYD's Flash Charging system is poised to set a new standard for EV charging.
