## 01 CATL Signs 60GWh Sodium-Ion Battery Deal with HyperStrong
CATL, the world's largest battery maker, has signed a massive 60 GWh sodium-ion battery deal with energy storage integrator HyperStrong. This agreement marks the largest sodium-ion battery order ever placed and is equivalent to half of all energy storage batteries CATL delivered in 2025. The deal signifies that CATL has overcome the challenges of mass-producing sodium-ion batteries, making them mainstream-ready. This development is crucial for the battery industry, as sodium-ion batteries offer a potentially more cost-effective and sustainable alternative to traditional lithium-ion batteries. With this deal, CATL is poised to lead the charge in the adoption of sodium-ion batteries for energy storage applications. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/04/27/catl-sodium-ion-battery-60gwh-energy-storage-deal/)

## 02 CATL Taps Hong Kong Market for $5 Billion to Fund Global Capacity Expansion
CATL has announced plans to raise $5 billion in the Hong Kong market to fund its global capacity expansion. This funding will support CATL's plans to increase production of sodium-ion batteries, as well as other battery technologies. The expansion is expected to help CATL maintain its position as the world's largest battery maker, while also driving the adoption of more sustainable and efficient battery technologies. With the growing demand for energy storage and electric vehicles, CATL's expansion plans are well-timed to meet the increasing needs of the market. [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/04/28/catl-taps-hk-market-5-billion-fund-capacity-expansion/)

## 03 BYD Denza Launches Second-Gen D9 with Ultra-Fast Charging to Defend MPV Market Share
BYD's Denza brand has launched the second-generation D9 MPV, featuring ultra-fast charging capabilities. The new D9 is designed to defend BYD's market share in the competitive EV sector, particularly in the MPV segment. With its ultra-fast charging capabilities, the D9 is poised to attract consumers looking for convenient and efficient charging solutions. The launch of the D9 is also significant for BYD, as it demonstrates the company's commitment to innovation and its ability to adapt to changing market trends. As the EV market continues to evolve, BYD's focus on ultra-fast charging and other advanced technologies is likely to remain a key differentiator for the company.
