## 01 Solid-State Batteries Now Powering EVs in the Real World
Solid-state EV batteries are now being tested on the road in North America for the first time, promising longer range, faster charging, and lower costs. This development marks a significant milestone in the adoption of solid-state battery technology, which has the potential to revolutionize the electric vehicle industry. With solid-state batteries, EVs can achieve longer ranges, faster charging times, and improved safety. According to [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/06/11/solid-state-batteries-now-powering-evs-in-real-world/), the testing of solid-state batteries in real-world conditions is a crucial step towards their widespread adoption.

## 02 Dodge Charger EV Prototype Packs Experimental Semi-Solid-State Battery
Dodge parent Stellantis is exploring the viability of semi-solid-state batteries with real-world testing in a Charger EV prototype. The experimental battery is being used to evaluate its performance, range, and charging capabilities. As reported by [InsideEVs](https://insideevs.com/news/798496/dodge-charger-daytona-ev-solid-state-battery/), the use of semi-solid-state batteries in the Charger EV prototype demonstrates the company's commitment to advancing battery technology and improving the overall performance of its electric vehicles.

## 03 BYD Converts Popular Electric Hatch into Van with Class-Leading Range for $40,000
BYD launched a purely electric van based on its popular Dolphin hatchback, offering a class-leading range for $40,000. The new van is designed to provide a practical and affordable solution for customers looking for an electric vehicle with ample space and range. According to [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/06/04/byd-converts-ev-hatch-into-van-with-leading-range-for-40k/), the van's range and pricing make it an attractive option for those in the market for an electric vehicle. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/06/04/byd-converts-ev-hatch-into-van-with-leading-range-for-40k/) and [InsideEVs](https://insideevs.com/news/798473/byd-dolphin-g-phev-europe/) provide more information on BYD's latest offerings.
