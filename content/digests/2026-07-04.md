## 01 CATL Deploys 2,000th Battery Swap Station
CATL, a leading battery manufacturer, has achieved a significant milestone in its battery swap network. The CATL-backed Choco-SEB battery swap network has deployed its 2,000th swapping station, marking a major achievement in the company's efforts to promote electric vehicle (EV) adoption. This rapid growth is a testament to the increasing demand for convenient and efficient EV charging solutions. With an average of over 200 deployments per month in 2026, CATL is poised to play a major role in shaping the future of EV charging infrastructure. ## 02 Iberdrola Launches First Large-Scale Battery Project in the US
Iberdrola, a Spanish renewable energy and electricity giant, has launched its first large-scale battery project in the US. This move is part of the company's efforts to strengthen the grid and promote the adoption of renewable energy sources. The project highlights the growing importance of energy storage in the transition to a low-carbon economy. As utilities continue to invest in battery energy storage, we can expect to see significant advancements in the sector. ## 03 Moment Energy Opens World’s Largest Second-Life EV Battery Factory
Moment Energy has officially opened the world's largest second-life EV battery factory, bringing new battery energy storage manufacturing capacity online. The factory was announced just six weeks prior, demonstrating the company's ability to rapidly execute on its plans. This development has significant implications for the EV industry, as it enables the reuse of batteries from retired EVs, reducing waste and promoting sustainability.
