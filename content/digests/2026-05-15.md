## 01 MG4 Hits 100,000 Units with Semi-Solid-State Battery
The MG4, the world's first mass-produced EV with a semi-solid-state battery, has quickly become a bestseller in China. In just eight months, the 100,000th model has rolled off the production line. This achievement is significant for the battery industry, as it demonstrates the growing demand for electric vehicles with advanced battery technologies. The semi-solid-state battery used in the MG4 offers improved safety, energy density, and charging speeds compared to traditional lithium-ion batteries. As the EV market continues to expand, the adoption of semi-solid-state batteries is expected to increase, driving innovation and investment in the sector. ## 02 ESS Expands into Short-Duration Storage with 8.5 GWh of Sodium-Ion Batteries
ESS, a leading provider of long-duration energy storage solutions, is expanding its portfolio to include short- and medium-duration battery storage systems. The company has added 8.5 GWh of sodium-ion batteries to its offerings, marking a significant shift in its strategy. Sodium-ion batteries offer a promising alternative to lithium-ion batteries, with potential advantages in terms of cost, sustainability, and performance. ESS's move into the short-duration storage market with sodium-ion technology is expected to increase competition and drive innovation in the industry. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/06/ess-adds-8-5-gwh-of-sodium-ion-to-its-battery-storage-portfolio/)

## 03 BYD's Yiwei Lithium Increases Registered Capital for Intelligent Robot Company
Yiwei Lithium, a subsidiary of BYD, has increased the registered capital of its intelligent robot company to 33 million yuan. This investment suggests that BYD is expanding its presence in the robotics sector, potentially leveraging its expertise in battery technology and electric vehicles. The growth of the intelligent robot company may lead to the development of new applications and products that integrate BYD's battery solutions, further diversifying the company's portfolio.
