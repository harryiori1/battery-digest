## 01 MG4 Hits 100,000 Sales with Semi-Solid-State Battery Option
The MG4, the world’s first mass-produced EV with a semi-solid-state battery, has quickly become a bestseller in China. In just eight months, the 100,000th model has rolled off the production line. This achievement is significant for the battery industry, as it demonstrates the growing demand for electric vehicles with advanced battery technologies. The semi-solid-state battery in the MG4 offers improved safety, energy density, and charging speeds compared to traditional lithium-ion batteries. As the EV market continues to expand, the success of the MG4 could pave the way for wider adoption of semi-solid-state batteries in the industry. ## 02 BYD Sees Sales Climb with Battery Capacity Ramp-Up
BYD has seen its monthly sales climb as the company ramps up its battery production capacity. This increase in sales is a direct result of BYD’s efforts to expand its battery manufacturing capabilities, which has enabled the company to meet growing demand for its electric vehicles. As one of the largest EV manufacturers in China, BYD’s ability to scale up its battery production will have a significant impact on the global EV market. With its battery capacity ramp-up, BYD is well-positioned to maintain its competitive edge in the industry and continue to drive growth in the EV sector. [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/05/15/byd-sees-sales-climbing-on-battery-ramp-up/)

## 03 Alsym Partners with Juniper for 500 MWh of Sodium-Ion Battery Storage
Alsym Energy has partnered with Juniper to bring 500 MWh of sodium-ion energy storage systems to California and other states. This partnership marks a significant milestone in the development of sodium-ion battery technology, which offers a potentially more cost-effective and sustainable alternative to traditional lithium-ion batteries. The deployment of 500 MWh of sodium-ion battery storage will help to address the growing demand for energy storage solutions, particularly in the context of renewable energy integration and grid resilience. As the energy storage market continues to evolve, the success of sodium-ion battery technology could have a profound impact on the industry. [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/05/13/alsym-partners-with-juniper-for-500-mwh-of-sodium-ion-grid-scale-battery-storage/)
