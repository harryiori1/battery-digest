BYD has unveiled its new Qin Max EV, featuring Flash Charging technology, which was spotted for the first time in public. With its Flash Charging capability, the Qin Max is expected to provide faster charging times, making it more convenient for users. This new model is part of BYD's efforts to expand its electric vehicle lineup and provide more options for consumers. ## 02 Sila Raises $300 Million to Scale Silicon Anode Production
Sila, a battery startup, has raised $300 million to scale up its silicon anode production. Sila's silicon anodes promise to deliver 20% more range for electric vehicles, making them an attractive option for automakers. The company is already working with major players like Mercedes and Panasonic, and this funding will help it increase production to meet growing demand. Sila's technology has the potential to significantly improve the performance and efficiency of electric vehicles, which could help drive adoption and reduce range anxiety. As the electric vehicle market continues to evolve, innovations like Sila's silicon anodes will be crucial in enabling the widespread adoption of electric vehicles. [InsideEVs](https://insideevs.com/news/802397/sila-silicon-anode-lithium-ion/)

## 03 Isuzu to Implement Swappable Battery Tech for Garbage Collection Route
Isuzu has partnered with Yokohama to test a three-minute battery-swapping system for municipal electric garbage trucks. This technology aims to eliminate lengthy charging delays for high-demand commercial vehicles, making it an attractive solution for urban areas. The swappable battery system will allow garbage trucks to quickly replace depleted batteries with fully charged ones, reducing downtime and increasing efficiency. This innovation has the potential to transform the way commercial vehicles operate in urban environments, enabling the widespread adoption of electric vehicles in industries like waste management. As cities around the world strive to reduce emissions and improve air quality, technologies like swappable battery systems will play a vital role in achieving these goals.
