## 01 GAC-Backed Greater Bay Claims Breakthrough in Solid-State Batteries
GAC-backed Greater Bay has announced a significant breakthrough in solid-state battery technology with the roll-out of a new prototype. This development has the potential to revolutionize the energy storage industry by providing safer, more efficient, and more sustainable solutions. Solid-state batteries replace the liquid electrolyte in traditional lithium-ion batteries with a solid material, enhancing stability and reducing the risk of fires. The new prototype demonstrates significant advancements in this technology, paving the way for its potential application in electric vehicles and other energy storage systems. As reported by [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/04/14/greater-bay-breakthrough-solid-state-batteries/), this breakthrough is a crucial step towards the commercialization of solid-state batteries.

## 02 US Researchers Develop Safer Water-Based Zinc-Ion Batteries
Researchers in the US have developed a new type of water-based zinc-ion battery that boasts a cycle life of 900 times, significantly improving upon existing zinc-ion battery technologies. This development is crucial for creating more sustainable and safer energy storage solutions. Zinc-ion batteries are considered a promising alternative to lithium-ion batteries due to their potential for lower costs, improved safety, and reduced environmental impact. The achievement of a 900-time cycle life indicates a substantial leap forward in the durability and reliability of these batteries, making them more viable for practical applications. As detailed by [第一电动](https://www.d1ev.com/news/shichang/295138), this breakthrough could pave the way for the widespread adoption of zinc-ion batteries in various sectors.
