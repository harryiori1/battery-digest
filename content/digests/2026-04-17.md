## 01 Geely Recalls 1,473 Polestar 4 EVs in China Over Battery Thermal Runaway Risk
Geely is recalling 1,473 Polestar 4 EVs in China due to a potential battery thermal runaway risk, according to a report by CnEVPost. The recall is a precautionary measure to ensure the safety of vehicle owners and passengers. Battery thermal runaway can occur when a battery cell overheats, causing a chain reaction that can lead to a fire. The recall highlights the importance of battery safety in electric vehicles and the need for manufacturers to prioritize the development of safe and reliable battery technologies. Geely will replace the affected battery packs free of charge, and owners can contact their local dealerships for more information. ## 03 BYD Reaches 16 Millionth NEV Production Milestone with New Denza D9
BYD has reached a significant production milestone, with its 16 millionth new energy vehicle rolling off the assembly line.
