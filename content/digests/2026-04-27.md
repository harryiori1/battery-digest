## 01 BYD Unveils Seal 08 with Blade Battery 2.0
BYD has unveiled its new flagship electric sedan, the Seal 08, at the Beijing Auto Show. The vehicle features BYD's second-generation Blade Battery, which targets over 1,000 km of CLTC range and megawatt-level flash charging that adds 400 km of range in five minutes. This marks the first time the Blade Battery 2.0 and flash charging combination have been used in a full-size sedan, positioning it as a direct competitor to other high-performance EVs. The Seal 08's advanced battery technology and charging capabilities make it an attractive option for those seeking a high-range and high-performance EV. ## 02 Tianqi Lithium Sees 1699.12% Year-on-Year Increase in Net Profit
Tianqi Lithium has announced a significant increase in its net profit for the first quarter of 2026, reaching 1.876 billion yuan. This represents a 1699.12% year-on-year increase, driven by a 98.44% increase in revenue, which totaled 51.28 billion yuan. The company's strong financial performance is likely due to the growing demand for lithium, a key component in battery production. As the battery industry continues to expand, companies like Tianqi Lithium are well-positioned to benefit from the increasing demand for lithium and other battery materials. ## 03 Ningde Era Partners with Houbolt for 60GWh Sodium Battery Energy Storage
Ningde Era has signed a 3-year cooperation agreement with Houbolt for 60GWh of sodium battery energy storage. This partnership aims to promote the industrialization of sodium batteries, which are seen as a potential alternative to traditional lithium-ion batteries. The use of sodium batteries in energy storage applications could help to reduce costs and increase efficiency, making them an attractive option for companies seeking to transition to more sustainable energy solutions. The cooperation between Ningde Era and Houbolt is expected to drive the development of sodium battery technology and accelerate its adoption in the industry.
