## 01 Aulton Revives Hong Kong IPO Bid for Battery Swap Specialist
Aulton, a battery swap specialist, has revived its bid for an initial public offering (IPO) in Hong Kong. This move aims to raise capital and become the first listed company specializing in battery swap technology. The development of battery swap systems is crucial for the widespread adoption of electric vehicles, as it provides a convenient and efficient way to recharge batteries. Aulton's IPO bid is significant, as it highlights the growing importance of battery swap technology in the electric vehicle industry. According to [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/07/20/aulton-revives-hk-ipo-bid/), Aulton's IPO bid is a notable development in the battery swap sector.

## 02 Cradle-to-Crisis Approach Improves Battery Safety and Performance
Researchers have emphasized the importance of adopting a "cradle-to-crisis" approach to improve battery safety and performance. This approach involves understanding the behavior of battery materials from their initial design to their potential failure points. By anticipating how new battery materials behave, manufacturers can design safer and more efficient batteries. As reported by [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/07/20/cradle-to-crisis-approach-improves-battery-safety-performance/), this approach is crucial in preventing thermal runaway and improving overall battery safety.

## 03 Chery Rhino Battery Passes Six Extreme Tests Without Replacement or Repair
Chery's Rhino battery has successfully passed six extreme tests, demonstrating its durability and performance. The tests, which included extreme temperatures and physical stress, were conducted without any replacement or repair of the battery. This achievement highlights the advancements in battery technology and the growing reliability of electric vehicle batteries. According to [第一电动](https://www.d1ev.com/news/qiye/307735), Chery's Rhino battery has shown impressive resilience and performance in extreme conditions.
