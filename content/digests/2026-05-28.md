## 01 BYD Struggles to Meet Demand for New Fast-Charging EVs
BYD is facing challenges in meeting the demand for its new flagship electric SUV and Song Ultra EV, both of which are equipped with the company's new Blade Battery 2.0 and Flash Charging technology. The new flagship electric SUV has received over 100,000 orders, while the Song Ultra EV has secured over 61,000 orders. Despite the strong demand, BYD has stated that it is struggling to keep up with production, highlighting the need for increased manufacturing capacity. This surge in demand is a testament to the growing popularity of BYD's electric vehicles, particularly those featuring its advanced battery and charging technologies. As the company works to expand its production capabilities, it is likely that we will see further investments in its manufacturing infrastructure. ## 02 BYD's New Blade Battery 2.0 Enables 5-Minute Flash Charging
BYD has successfully demonstrated the capabilities of its new Blade Battery 2.0 and Flash Charging technology by completing a 2,700-mile trip across China's largest expressway in its Song Ultra EV. This achievement showcases the potential of BYD's advanced battery and charging technologies, which enable fast and efficient recharging. The Song Ultra EV was able to complete the trip without any issues, highlighting the reliability and performance of BYD's new battery and charging system. This development is significant, as it underscores the potential for electric vehicles to undertake long-distance trips without the need for lengthy recharging periods. As the technology continues to evolve, we can expect to see further improvements in the range and charging capabilities of electric vehicles. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/20/byd-ev-conquers-2700-mile-trip-with-new-battery-5-min-charging/)

## 03 BYD's Denza Z9GT Achieves 97% Charge in 12 Minutes at -22 Degrees
The Denza Z9GT, powered by BYD's second-generation Blade Battery, has achieved an impressive 97% charge in just 12 minutes, even in extreme cold temperatures of -22 degrees. This demonstration highlights the capabilities of BYD's advanced battery technology, which enables fast and efficient recharging even in challenging environmental conditions. The Denza Z9GT's performance is a testament to the reliability and performance of BYD's battery and charging system, which is designed to provide a seamless and efficient recharging experience. As electric vehicles become increasingly popular, the development of advanced battery and charging technologies will play a critical role in shaping the industry's future.
