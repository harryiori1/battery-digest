## 01 CATL Develops LFP EV Battery with 6-Minute Recharge Time
CATL has developed a new lithium iron phosphate (LFP) EV battery that can be fully recharged in just 6 minutes, beating out BYD's Blade Battery 2.0. This breakthrough in battery technology could significantly impact the EV industry, enabling faster and more efficient charging. The new battery's rapid recharge time is expected to improve the overall driving experience and increase the adoption of electric vehicles. According to [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/04/21/catl-one-ups-byd-new-lfp-ev-battery-charges-in-6-mins/), CATL's innovative battery design has achieved a major milestone in the development of high-performance EV batteries.

## 02 Chinese Scientists Claim Breakthrough in Iron Flow Storage Batteries
Scientists in China have made a significant breakthrough in iron flow battery technology, which could lead to the development of cheaper and more efficient grid-scale storage batteries. According to [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/04/28/chinese-iron-flow-storage-battery-is-80-times-cheaper-than-lithium/), the new iron flow battery design is 80 times cheaper than traditional lithium batteries, making it an attractive alternative for large-scale energy storage applications. This innovation has the potential to revolutionize the energy storage industry, enabling wider adoption of renewable energy sources and reducing our reliance on fossil fuels.
