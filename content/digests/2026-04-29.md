## 01 CATL Inks Deal For 60 GWh Of Sodium Energy Storage Batteries
CATL has partnered with HyperStrong to supply 60 GWh of sodium-ion battery cells for energy storage systems over the next three years. This deal marks a significant milestone in the development of sodium-ion batteries, which are considered a potential alternative to lithium-ion batteries. The partnership aims to promote the application of sodium-ion batteries in the energy storage sector, providing a more sustainable and cost-effective solution. According to [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/04/29/catl-inks-deal-for-60-gwh-of-sodium-energy-storage-batteries-over-next-three-years/), the deal is expected to drive the growth of the energy storage market, with sodium-ion batteries offering a promising solution for large-scale energy storage applications.

## 02 BYD’s Song Ultra EV Secures 60,000 Orders with Blade Battery and Flash Charging
BYD's new Song Ultra EV has received over 60,000 orders in one month, starting at $22,000. The electric SUV is equipped with BYD's Blade Battery and Flash Charging technology, enabling recharge times in as little as 5 minutes. As reported by [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/04/29/byds-new-song-ultra-ev-racks-up-60000-orders-in-one-month/), the Song Ultra EV's success can be attributed to its competitive pricing, advanced battery technology, and fast charging capabilities. The Blade Battery, in particular, has been designed to provide improved safety, efficiency, and range, making it an attractive option for consumers.

## 03 Chinese Iron Flow Storage Battery Is 80 Times Cheaper Than Lithium
Chinese scientists have made a breakthrough in iron flow battery technology, potentially leading to cheaper grid-scale storage batteries. According to [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/04/28/chinese-iron-flow-storage-battery-is-80-times-cheaper-than-lithium/), the new iron flow battery is 80 times cheaper than lithium-ion batteries, making it a promising solution for large-scale energy storage applications. The technology has the potential to reduce the cost of energy storage, making renewable energy sources more viable and contributing to a more sustainable energy mix. However, further research and development are needed to commercialize the technology and overcome existing challenges.
