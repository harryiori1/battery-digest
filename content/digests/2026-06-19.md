## 01 Graphite One to Onshore Graphite Supply for EV Batteries in the US
Graphite One, a Canadian firm, has announced plans to onshore the graphite supply for EV batteries and other energy storage applications in the US. The company intends to mine graphite in Alaska and process it in Ohio, aiming to support the growing US EV battery ecosystem. This move is expected to reduce reliance on imported graphite and enhance the domestic supply chain. According to [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/06/19/ev-battery-supply-chain-us-graphite-alaska-ohio/), Graphite One's initiative is a significant addition to the US EV battery supply chain.
