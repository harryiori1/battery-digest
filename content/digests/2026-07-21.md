## 01 CATL Signs 2 GWh Sodium Battery Storage Deal with Europe's Solarpro
CATL has signed a significant deal with Europe's Solarpro to provide 2 GWh of sodium battery storage. This marks a major step in the adoption of sodium-ion batteries, which promise lower costs, improved safety, and reduced dependence on critical minerals like lithium, cobalt, and nickel. The deal is a testament to CATL's commitment to innovation and its leadership in the battery industry. As the world transitions to renewable energy, sodium-ion batteries are expected to play a crucial role in energy storage. ## 02 UNIGRID’s Sodium-Ion Home Battery Debuts in Europe, US is Next
UNIGRID has made a significant breakthrough in the home battery market with the debut of its Na+Casa residential battery in Europe. The company has shipped the first units, with the first systems now installed in homes across Europe. UNIGRID plans to expand to the US market next, marking a major step in the adoption of sodium-ion batteries in the home energy storage sector. The Na+Casa battery offers a cost-effective and efficient solution for homeowners looking to store excess energy generated by their solar panels. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/07/10/unigrid-sodium-ion-home-battery-debuts-in-europe-us/)

## 03 Janus Electric to Convert 67 Diesel Trucks to Battery-Swap EVs
Janus Electric has received an order to convert 67 Class 8 heavy-duty freight and drayage trucks from diesels to advanced, battery-swap electric vehicles. The conversion will enable the trucks to operate with zero emissions, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and improving air quality. The battery-swap technology allows for quick and efficient replacement of batteries, minimizing downtime and increasing productivity. The deal marks a significant step in the adoption of electric vehicles in the heavy-duty trucking industry.
