## 02 Tesla Adds Sunwoda as Fifth Global EV Battery Supplier
Tesla has added Sunwoda, a Chinese battery manufacturer, as its fifth global EV battery supplier. This move is part of Tesla's efforts to diversify its supply chain and reduce costs. With the addition of Sunwoda, Tesla aims to improve its margins, which have been under pressure due to rising production costs. The company has already started shipping vehicles with Sunwoda's LFP cells to export markets. This partnership is expected to have a significant impact on the EV industry, as Tesla continues to lead the way in innovative and sustainable transportation solutions. As the demand for EVs continues to grow, Tesla's strategic move to diversify its supply chain will likely pay off in the long run. ## 03 BYD Partners with KFC to Offer 9-Minute EV Charging
BYD has partnered with KFC to offer a convenient one-stop shop and charge service for EV drivers. The partnership allows customers to charge their vehicles in just 9 minutes while they grab a bite to eat. This innovative service is expected to attract more customers to KFC, while also promoting the adoption of EVs. As the world shifts towards more sustainable modes of transportation, partnerships like this one will play a crucial role in making EVs more accessible and convenient for the masses. With BYD's expertise in EV technology and KFC's vast network of locations, this partnership has the potential to revolutionize the way we think about EV charging.
