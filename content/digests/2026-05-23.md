## 02 Gotion Unveils Sodium Battery Products With 261 Wh/kg Energy Density
Gotion has introduced three new sodium battery products with high energy density and long charge cycles. The new products have an energy density of 261 Wh/kg and can withstand up to 20,000 charge cycles. This development is significant, as sodium batteries are considered a promising alternative to traditional lithium-ion batteries. Sodium is abundant and inexpensive, making it an attractive option for large-scale energy storage applications. Gotion's new products are expected to have a significant impact on the energy storage industry, as they provide a more sustainable and cost-effective solution.
