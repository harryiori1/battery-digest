## 01 BYD Expands Battery Production in Brazil
BYD has announced the expansion of its battery production in Brazil, aiming to support the growing demand for electric vehicles (EVs) in the region. The company's vehicle exports have grown by 65% this year, with an 80% increase in May alone. This move is part of BYD's strategy to increase sales outside of China and return to EV sales growth. The Brazilian plant will play a crucial role in supplying batteries to BYD's EV manufacturing facilities in the region. With this expansion, BYD is poised to strengthen its position in the global EV market. [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/06/20/byd-battery-production-ramps-up-in-brazil/)

## 02 India Requires 10 GWh of Battery Storage
India is facing a significant challenge in integrating renewable energy into its power grid due to the inflexibility of coal-based power plants. To address this issue, the country requires approximately 10 gigawatt-hours (GWh) of battery storage immediately. This would enable the grid to accommodate the midday solar surge and reduce renewable energy curtailment. The implementation of battery storage would not only help mitigate the waste of clean power but also support India's transition to a more sustainable energy mix. [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/06/20/india-needs-10-gwh-of-battery-storage-now-to-stop-coals-inflexibility-wasting-clean-power/)
