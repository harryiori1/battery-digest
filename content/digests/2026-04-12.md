## 03 Home Batteries Offer More Than Just Backup Power
Home batteries are often seen as a means of providing backup power during outages, but they can offer much more than that. In addition to backup power, home batteries can provide energy shifting and load shifting, allowing homeowners to optimize their energy usage and reduce their reliance on the grid. This can be especially beneficial for homeowners with solar panels, as they can store excess energy generated during the day for use at night. With the growing adoption of home batteries, homeowners are beginning to realize the full potential of these systems and are using them to take control of their energy usage.
