## 01 Huawei HIMA Brings in Battery Suppliers Beyond CATL
Huawei's electric vehicle arm, HIMA, is diversifying its battery supply chain to reduce costs. According to a report by CnEVPost, HIMA is bringing in battery suppliers beyond CATL, which has been the sole supplier of batteries to Huawei's electric vehicles. This move is seen as a cost-cutting measure, as HIMA aims to reduce its reliance on a single supplier and negotiate better prices with multiple suppliers. The report cites industry sources, who reveal that HIMA is in talks with several battery suppliers, including Guoxuan High-Tech and other second-tier suppliers. This development is significant, as it highlights the growing importance of battery supply chain diversification in the electric vehicle industry. [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/06/16/huawei-hima-brings-in-battery-suppliers-beyond-catl/)

## 02 Tesla Cybercab Specs Revealed: 48 kWh Battery, 219 HP
The technical specifications of Tesla's robotaxi, the Cybercab, have been revealed through EPA certification documents. According to a report by Electrek, the Cybercab has a 48 kWh battery pack, a 219 HP motor, and a curb weight of 3,113 lbs. The vehicle's range is estimated to be around 250 miles, although this has not been officially confirmed by Tesla. The Cybercab is a significant development in the electric vehicle industry, as it represents Tesla's entry into the robotaxi market. The vehicle's specs suggest that it will be a competitive player in this market, with a balance of range, performance, and efficiency. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/06/15/tesla-cybercab-epa-specs-curb-weight-battery-motor-power/)

## 03 CATL's Net Profit Expected to Exceed 3.3 Billion Yuan in First Half
CATL, the Chinese battery giant, is expected to see significant profit growth in the first half of the year. According to a report by 第一电动, CATL's net profit is expected to exceed 3.3 billion yuan (around $460 million) in the first half of 2026. This represents a significant increase from the same period last year, and is attributed to the growing demand for electric vehicles and energy storage systems. CATL is one of the largest battery suppliers in the world, and its financial performance is closely watched by industry analysts. The company's expected profit growth is seen as a positive sign for the electric vehicle industry, which is experiencing rapid growth and adoption. [第一电动](https://www.d1ev.com/news/shichang/303421)
