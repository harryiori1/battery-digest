## 01 Svolt Begins Mass Production of 80-kWh High-Capacity PHEV Battery
Svolt, a leading Chinese battery manufacturer, has begun mass production of its 80-kWh high-capacity plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV) battery. This development marks a significant milestone in the company's efforts to create high-performance battery solutions for the automotive industry. The 80-kWh battery is designed to provide extended electric range and improved fuel efficiency for PHEVs, making it an attractive option for manufacturers and consumers alike. With this achievement, Svolt is poised to enhance its competitiveness in the global battery market and further solidify its position as a major player in the industry. [Svolt](https://cnevpost.com/2026/04/16/svolt-begins-mass-production-80-kwh-phev-battery/).

## 02 Dangsheng Technology to Invest in Integrated Lithium Iron Phosphate Project
Dangsheng Technology, a Chinese company, plans to purchase equity for 145 million yuan and invest in an integrated lithium iron phosphate project. The project includes a 150,000-ton per year lithium iron phosphate production line and a 200,000-ton per year high-performance lithium iron phosphate battery material project. This investment aims to capitalize on the growing demand for lithium iron phosphate batteries, which are known for their safety, stability, and environmental benefits. By expanding its presence in the lithium iron phosphate market, Dangsheng Technology is well-positioned to benefit from the increasing adoption of electric vehicles and renewable energy systems. [Dangsheng Technology](https://36kr.com/newsflashes/3769414422037255).

## 03 BYD's Chief Scientist Says Solid-State EV Batteries Hit a 'Critical Stage'
BYD's chief scientist, Lian Yubo, recently stated that solid-state EV batteries have entered a "critical stage." This announcement indicates significant progress in the development of solid-state battery technology, which promises to offer improved safety, energy density, and charging speeds compared to traditional lithium-ion batteries. As a leading manufacturer of electric vehicles and battery solutions, BYD is at the forefront of solid-state battery research and development. The company's advancements in this field are expected to have a profound impact on the electric vehicle industry, enabling the creation of more efficient, reliable, and sustainable vehicles. [BYD](https://electrek.co/2026/04/08/byds-chief-scientist-solid-state-ev-batteries-critical-stage/).
