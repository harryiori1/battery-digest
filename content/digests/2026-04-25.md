## 02 BAIC Group Deepens Strategic Cooperation with CATL
BAIC Group, a leading Chinese automaker, has strengthened its strategic partnership with CATL, a prominent battery manufacturer. The cooperation aims to enhance BAIC Group's electric vehicle capabilities and accelerate its transition to new energy vehicles. The partnership will focus on the development of advanced battery technologies, including solid-state batteries and lithium-ion batteries with improved performance and safety characteristics. CATL will provide BAIC Group with cutting-edge battery solutions, while BAIC Group will share its expertise in electric vehicle design and manufacturing. ## 03 Tesla Lithium Refinery Discharge Contains Toxic Metals, Faces Cease-and-Desist Order
Tesla's lithium refinery in Robstown, Texas, has been found to discharge wastewater containing toxic metals, including hexavalent chromium and arsenic. The Nueces County Drainage District No. 2, which manages the ditch receiving Tesla's 231,000-gallon daily discharge, has issued a cease-and-desist letter demanding the company to halt its refinery operations. Independent lab testing revealed that the wastewater contains elevated levels of lithium, as well as traces of other toxic substances. The discovery has raised concerns about the environmental impact of Tesla's lithium refinery and the potential risks to local communities. Tesla must address the issue and implement measures to prevent further contamination of the water supply.
