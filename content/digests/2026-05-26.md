## 01 Zhong Manzi Invests $500 Million in Solid-State Batteries

This significant investment aims to improve the energy density and safety of solid-state batteries, which are considered a crucial technology for the future of electric vehicles. The investment will focus on research and development, with the goal of achieving a higher energy density and reducing production costs. ## 02 NIO's Long-Term Battery Swapping Strategy
NIO, a Chinese electric vehicle manufacturer, has announced its long-term strategy for battery swapping. The company prioritizes user experience and network expansion over short-term profitability in its battery swapping business. NIO's battery swapping service allows users to swap their batteries at designated stations, providing a convenient and efficient way to recharge their vehicles. The company plans to continue expanding its battery swapping network, with a focus on improving user experience and reducing costs. This strategy is expected to have a positive impact on the adoption of electric vehicles, as it addresses one of the major concerns of potential buyers: range anxiety.
