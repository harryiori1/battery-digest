## 01 BYD's New Yuan Plus Secures Over 30,000 Orders in First Week
BYD's new electric SUV, the Yuan Plus, has secured over 30,000 orders in its first week of sales. The vehicle features a longer driving range and flash charging capabilities, which allow it to recharge from 10% to 70% in just 5 minutes. This rapid charging technology is made possible by BYD's new Blade Battery 2.0, which enables faster and more efficient charging. The Yuan Plus is sold overseas as the Atto 3 and is among BYD's best-selling vehicles. The strong demand for the new model is a testament to the growing popularity of electric vehicles and the importance of advanced battery technology in driving their adoption. ## 02 BYD's 5-Minute Charging Luxury EV Deliveries Top 10,000
BYD's luxury electric vehicle, the Denza Z9 GT, has delivered over 10,000 units since its launch. The vehicle features a 5-minute charging capability, which allows it to recharge from 10% to 90% in just 9 minutes. This rapid charging technology is made possible by BYD's new Blade Battery 2.0 and is a key factor in the vehicle's popularity. The Denza Z9 GT is also equipped with advanced autonomous driving capabilities and a range of luxury features. The strong sales of the vehicle demonstrate the growing demand for high-end electric vehicles with advanced battery technology. ## 03 Industrial Accelerator Act to Help Avoid Battery Factory Busts
The Industrial Accelerator Act is a new law aimed at supporting the development of local battery industries and preventing bankruptcies. The law provides funding and resources for battery manufacturers to invest in research and development, improve efficiency, and reduce costs. This is particularly important for the battery industry, where companies are struggling to keep up with growing demand and increasing competition. The law is expected to help avoid more battery factory busts, such as the recent bankruptcy of Morrow Batteries, and support the growth of a sustainable and competitive battery industry. [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/05/29/how-the-industrial-accelerator-act-can-help-avoid-more-battery-factories-going-bust/)
