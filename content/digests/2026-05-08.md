## 01 Hyundai Patent Reveals New Body-on-Frame EV Battery Setup
Hyundai is preparing to launch its first body-on-frame vehicle, a midsize pickup truck, with a new battery and chassis setup. The rugged Boulder Concept SUV is a preview of the upcoming vehicle, but a new patent offers a sneak peek at the battery and chassis setup. The patent reveals a unique battery layout, with the battery pack integrated into the frame of the vehicle. This design allows for a more efficient use of space and improved weight distribution. The new battery setup is expected to provide improved range and performance for the upcoming vehicle. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/07/hyundai-patent-reveals-new-body-on-frame-ev-battery-setup/)

## 02 Tesla’s 4680 Battery Cells Underperforming and Frustrating Buyers
Tesla's homemade 4680 battery cells have been underperforming and frustrating buyers. The data shows that the 4680 cells deliver worse energy density, charging performance, and range compared to supplier cells. This is a significant issue for Tesla, as the company has been promoting the 4680 cells as a key component of its electric vehicles. The underperformance of the 4680 cells has led to concerns about the company's ability to produce high-quality battery cells. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/07/tesla-4680-battery-cell-performance-data-shows-cant-build-own-cells/)

## 03 Nio Provides 1 Million Battery Swaps During May Day Holiday
Nio's battery swap technology has provided over 1 million swaps during the May Day holiday in China. The company's battery swap stations have been popular among electric vehicle owners, who can quickly and easily swap out their depleted batteries for fully charged ones. The success of Nio's battery swap technology has helped to alleviate range anxiety and make electric vehicles more convenient for owners. [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/05/07/nio-provides-1-million-battery-swaps-may-day-holiday/)
