## 01 CATL and Octopus Energy Form Swaptopus Joint Venture for Automated Battery Swapping
CATL, a leading battery manufacturer, has partnered with Octopus Energy to form Swaptopus, a joint venture aimed at developing automated battery-swapping hubs for heavy trucks across Europe. This initiative marks a significant step in the region's electric vehicle adoption, particularly in the commercial sector. The partnership will leverage CATL's expertise in battery technology and Octopus Energy's experience in energy retail and technology to create a comprehensive solution for electric truck operators. With the European Union's stringent emissions regulations and growing demand for sustainable transportation, the Swaptopus joint venture is poised to play a crucial role in the transition to electric vehicles. As the demand for electric trucks continues to rise, CATL's investment in the Swaptopus platform demonstrates its commitment to supporting the growth of the European electric vehicle market. ## 02 BYD Teases 1,008-km Da Han EV Ahead of Chengdu Auto Show Debut
BYD has unveiled its upcoming Da Han electric vehicle, boasting an impressive range of 1,008 kilometers. The Da Han EV is set to make its debut at the Chengdu Auto Show in August, and its exceptional range is expected to generate significant interest among Chinese consumers. The vehicle's advanced battery technology and efficient powertrain enable it to achieve remarkable mileage, making it an attractive option for those seeking a reliable and long-range electric vehicle. As BYD continues to expand its product lineup and improve its battery technology, the company is well-positioned to maintain its competitive edge in the Chinese electric vehicle market. With the Da Han EV, BYD aims to cater to the growing demand for electric vehicles in China, driven by government incentives and increasing consumer awareness of sustainable transportation options. The delivery of the 10,000th Da Tang EV demonstrates BYD's commitment to meeting the increasing demand for sustainable transportation options in China.
