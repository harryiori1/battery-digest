## 01 Rivian Partners with Redwood on Energy Storage
Rivian, the electric vehicle manufacturer, has partnered with Redwood Materials, a battery company founded by Tesla co-founder JB Straubel, to build a second-life battery energy storage system at its EV manufacturing plant in Normal, Illinois. The initial deployment will use more than 100 used Rivian battery packs stitched together into a 10 MWh system that can feed power back into the factory during peak demand. This partnership highlights the growing trend of repurposing electric vehicle batteries for energy storage applications, reducing waste and increasing the overall efficiency of the energy ecosystem. ## 02 CATL Sees Q1 Profit Climb 48.5% as Revenue Jumps Over 50%
CATL, the leading battery manufacturer, has reported a significant increase in profit and revenue for the first quarter of 2026. The company's net profit reached 20.738 billion yuan, representing a 48.52% year-over-year growth, while revenue jumped 52.45% to 1291.31 billion yuan. This strong performance is attributed to the growing demand for electric vehicles and energy storage systems, which has driven up the demand for CATL's battery products. The company's financial results demonstrate its position as a leader in the battery industry and its ability to capitalize on the trend towards electrification. ## 03 Ningde Era to Establish Era Resources Group with 30 Billion Yuan Registered Capital
Ningde Era, the parent company of CATL, has announced plans to establish a new subsidiary, Era Resources Group, with a registered capital of 30 billion yuan. The new company will focus on investing in and managing new energy mineral resources, aiming to secure a stable supply of key materials for CATL's battery production. This move marks a significant expansion of Ningde Era's business scope, as it seeks to integrate its operations upstream and downstream, enhancing its competitiveness in the global battery market.
