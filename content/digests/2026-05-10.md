## 01 BYD's Luxury Off-Road Brand Unveils First Electric Sedan and Sports Car
BYD's luxury off-road brand, Fang Cheng Bao, has unveiled its first electric sedan and a low-slung sports car, marking a significant expansion of its product line. The new electric sedan and Formula X sports car are designed to appeal to a new market, showcasing BYD's commitment to diversifying its offerings. This move is expected to increase competition in the luxury electric vehicle market, with BYD's sub-brand poised to challenge established players. According to [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/05/byds-off-road-brand-debuts-first-ev-sedan-sports-car-images/), the new models demonstrate BYD's ability to innovate and adapt to changing market trends.

## 02 BYD Sells Its Most Expensive EV Ever for Nearly $3 Million
BYD has sold its most expensive electric vehicle ever, the Yangwang U9 Xtreme, for nearly $3 million at the Beijing Auto Show. This electric supercar boasts an impressive 3,000 hp and has set a new record for BYD's most expensive vehicle sold. As reported by [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/01/byd-sells-most-expensive-ev-ever-for-3-million/), this sale highlights the growing demand for high-end electric vehicles and BYD's ability to cater to this niche market.
