## 01 World's First Solid-State Electric Motorcycle Enters Production
Verge Motorcycles announced that it has begun production of what it claims to be the world's first electric motorcycle powered by solid-state batteries. This marks a significant milestone in battery technology, moving solid-state solutions from research into commercial application in a vehicle. [InsideEVs](https://insideevs.com/news/791709/worlds-first-production-solid-state-battery-motorcycle/).

## 02 BYD Unveils New Seal Models with Ultra-Fast Charging
BYD has officially launched its 2026 Seal 06 GT and wagon models, which come equipped with advanced ultra-fast charging technology. This product launch highlights BYD's continuous innovation in EV technology and its strategic expansion into new vehicle segments like wagons. [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/04/02/byd-launches-2026-seal-06-gt-dm-i-wagon/) and [第一电动](https://www.d1ev.com/news/shichang/293994).

## 03 China's NEV Wholesale Sales Rebound Significantly in March
China's New Energy Vehicle (NEV) wholesale sales experienced a robust rebound in March, increasing by an estimated 55% from February. This strong performance indicates a significant recovery in the world's largest EV market, with various brands intensifying competition. [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/04/02/china-mar-nev-wholesale-cpca-estimates/) and [第一电动](https://www.d1ev.com/news/qiye/293962).
