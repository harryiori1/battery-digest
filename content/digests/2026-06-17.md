## 02 Donut Lab’s ‘Solid-State’ Battery Exposed as Regular Li-Ion in Damning Investigation
A recent investigation has exposed Donut Lab's claimed solid-state battery as a regular lithium-ion cell. The company had raised approximately $25 million from over 1,300 investors based on false claims about its battery technology. The investigation, which involved over 20 independent battery experts, found that Donut Lab's battery was not a solid-state battery as claimed, but rather a standard lithium-ion cell. This revelation has significant implications for the company and its investors, and raises concerns about the credibility of claims made by companies in the battery industry. The investigation highlights the importance of rigorous testing and verification of claims made by companies, particularly in the field of battery technology.
