## 01 Electrify America Opens Largest Battery-Backed Charging Station
Electrify America has opened its largest battery-backed charging station in downtown Santa Barbara, California. The station features 20 DC fast chargers and a large public battery energy storage system. This move is expected to support the growing demand for electric vehicles in the region. The battery energy storage system will help to stabilize the grid and provide a reliable source of power for the charging station. This development is significant for the battery industry as it highlights the importance of energy storage in supporting the adoption of electric vehicles. [Electrify America](https://electrek.co/2026/06/17/electrify-america-biggest-battery-storage-charging-hub/).

## 02 Ford Begins Building Low-Cost LFP Battery Cells in the US
Ford has started building low-cost LFP battery cells in the US, which will power its $30,000 EV pickup. This makes Ford the first company to use LFP batteries in a mainstream EV in the US. The use of LFP batteries is expected to reduce the cost of the vehicle and make it more competitive in the market. LFP batteries have several advantages, including lower costs, improved safety, and longer lifetimes. This development is significant for the battery industry as it highlights the growing adoption of LFP batteries in the EV market. [Ford](https://electrek.co/2026/06/17/ford-begins-building-low-cost-lfp-batteries-for-30k-ev-pickup/).

## 03 BYD Launches Flagship Electric SUV with Record 150,000 Preorders
BYD has launched its flagship electric SUV, which has received a record 150,000 preorders. The full-size luxury SUV has a range of nearly 600 miles and can recharge in as little as five minutes. The vehicle features advanced technology, including a high-performance motor and a state-of-the-art battery management system. This development is significant for the battery industry as it highlights the growing demand for high-performance electric vehicles. The use of advanced battery technology in the vehicle has enabled it to achieve a long range and fast charging times, making it an attractive option for consumers. [BYD](https://electrek.co/2026/06/17/byd-launches-flagship-ev-suv-record-150000-preorders/).
