## 01 ELM MicroGrid Installs Battery Storage in Its Hometown
ELM MicroGrid, a battery energy storage specialist, has installed a new battery energy storage system at a solar microgrid project in Peoria, IL. This project marks a significant milestone for the company, as it brings its expertise in energy storage and utility infrastructure to its hometown. The installation of the battery energy storage system is expected to provide a reliable and efficient source of power for the microgrid, and it demonstrates the growing importance of energy storage in the transition to renewable energy. According to [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/06/03/elm-microgrid-installs-battery-storage-in-its-hometown-video/), the company has spent over two decades building a nationwide energy storage and utility infrastructure business.

## 03 Volt Harbor Raises $2 Million In Funding for Energy Storage Technology
Volt Harbor, a Michigan-based battery energy storage technology startup, has raised $2 million in seed money, according to [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/06/02/energy-storage-technology-company-volt-harbor-raises-2-million-in-funding/). The company's modular, software-defined energy storage platform is designed for use in data centers and the grid, and it works with EV batteries, which are in abundant supply and growing in quantity. The funding will be used to further develop the company's technology and expand its operations. Volt Harbor's energy storage platform has the potential to play a significant role in the transition to renewable energy, and the company's innovative approach to energy storage is likely to attract further investment in the future.
