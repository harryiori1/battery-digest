## 01 Daqian Energy to Invest 6 Billion Yuan in Smart Energy System Manufacturing Base
Daqian Energy has announced plans to invest 6 billion yuan in building a smart energy system manufacturing base project. The project will engage in R&D and manufacturing of products including solid-state transformers and solid-state batteries. The investment will be made in two phases, with the first phase investing approximately 21 billion yuan. The project aims to optimize the company's business structure and cultivate new profit growth points. This investment is significant for the battery industry as it shows a commitment to developing new energy storage technologies, including solid-state batteries. ## 02 Volt Harbor Raises $2 Million In Funding for Energy Storage Technology
Volt Harbor, a Michigan-based battery energy storage technology startup, has raised $2 million in seed money. The company makes a modular, software-defined energy storage platform for data centers and the grid. Volt Harbor's technology works with EV batteries, which are in abundant supply and are growing in quantity. This funding will help the company to further develop its technology and expand its market reach. The use of EV batteries for energy storage is an innovative approach that can help to reduce waste and increase the efficiency of energy storage systems. ## 03 BYD's Cast Aluminum Frame is Lighter, Tougher, and Safer than Steel
BYD has announced that its cast aluminum frame is lighter, tougher, and safer than steel. The aluminum frame underpins BYD's new Yangwang U8L SUV and passes a brutal 12 ton lift test. The frame weighs over 100 lbs less than a comparable steel frame, making it a significant innovation in the automotive industry. This technology can help to improve the efficiency and safety of electric vehicles, which is crucial for the development of the battery industry.
