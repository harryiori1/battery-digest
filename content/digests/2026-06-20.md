## 03 Tesla Model Y Battery Test Reveals Significant Degradation
A real-world test of a Tesla Model Y's battery health revealed significant degradation, with the battery capacity dropping to 88% after just 20,000km. The test, which was conducted by a Tesla Model Y owner, raises concerns about the durability of Tesla's batteries. The significant degradation of the battery capacity after just 20,000km is a cause for concern, as it could impact the overall performance and range of the vehicle.
