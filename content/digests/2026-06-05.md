## 03 Limitations of Traditional Super Charging Stations
Traditional super charging stations are facing challenges in withstanding continuous high power charging, with liquid cooling emerging as a potential solution. The high power charging requirements of EVs are posing significant challenges for traditional super charging stations, which are not designed to handle the high power demands. Liquid cooling is being seen as a potential solution to this problem, with companies exploring new technologies to improve the efficiency and reliability of their charging systems.
