## 01 Svolt to Mass-Produce Hybrid Solid-Liquid Batteries by September
Svolt, a leading battery manufacturer, has announced plans to mass-produce hybrid solid-liquid batteries by September at liquid battery costs. This significant milestone in battery technology is expected to improve the efficiency and affordability of electric vehicles. The hybrid solid-liquid battery combines the benefits of solid-state batteries, such as enhanced safety and energy density, with the cost-effectiveness of liquid batteries. Svolt's innovative approach is likely to have a profound impact on the electric vehicle industry, enabling the widespread adoption of eco-friendly transportation. According to [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2026/05/19/svolt-plans-mass-produce-hybrid-solid-liquid-batteries-september-liquid-battery-costs/), Svolt's hybrid solid-liquid batteries will be produced at a cost comparable to traditional liquid batteries, making them an attractive option for electric vehicle manufacturers.

## 02 BYD's New Blade Battery Put to the Test with 2,700-Mile Drive
BYD, a leading electric vehicle manufacturer, is putting its new Blade Battery to the test with a 2,700-mile drive across China's largest expressway. The new Song Ultra EV, equipped with the Blade Battery and 5-minute Flash Charging tech, will become the first electric vehicle to complete the full trip. This ambitious undertaking demonstrates BYD's confidence in its new battery technology, which is designed to provide enhanced safety, range, and charging speed. As reported by [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/18/byd-drives-ev-2700-miles-to-prove-new-battery-5-min-charging/), the Blade Battery has undergone rigorous testing, including a grueling 8-hour teardown, to ensure its reliability and performance.

## 03 NLR Battery Innovation Awarded NASA’s Invention of the Year
NLR Battery Innovation has been awarded NASA's Invention of the Year, recognizing its significant contributions to the development of lithium-ion batteries for space exploration. The award highlights the importance of battery technology in enabling space missions and the potential for innovation in this field. As noted by [CleanTechnica](https://cleantechnica.com/2026/05/18/nlr-battery-innovation-awarded-nasas-invention-of-the-year/), NLR Battery Innovation's work has far-reaching implications for the electric vehicle industry and beyond, demonstrating the potential for battery technology to drive progress in various fields.
