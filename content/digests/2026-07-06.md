## 02 VC Price of Electrolyte Additives Soars, Affects Battery Production
The price of VC, a key electrolyte additive used in lithium-ion batteries, has soared in recent times. The increase in demand for VC has led to a shortage in supply, causing prices to rise. As a result, leading battery manufacturers such as CATL and BYD have begun to bind production capacity to secure a stable supply of VC. This development is expected to have a significant impact on the battery industry, with manufacturers facing increased costs and potential production delays.
