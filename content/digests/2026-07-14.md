## 02 NIO's New Battery Research and Development Base to be Built in Shanghai
NIO, a prominent Chinese electric vehicle manufacturer, is establishing a new battery research and development base in Jiading, Shanghai. The construction of the base is scheduled to start in the second half of the year, marking a significant expansion of NIO's battery research capabilities. This move is expected to enhance NIO's competitiveness in the EV market, as the company continues to invest in advanced battery technologies. The new base will likely play a crucial role in the development of NIO's future battery products. [第一电动](https://www.d1ev.com/news/shichang/306695)
