## 01 BYD Unveils New Yuan Plus EV with Enhanced Range and Fast Charging
BYD has launched the third-generation Yuan Plus EV, which offers nearly 400 miles of driving range and fast charging capabilities, starting at under $18,000. The upgraded electric SUV features BYD's new Flash Charging system, which enables rapid recharging. This development is significant for the battery industry, as it demonstrates BYD's commitment to improving the range and charging capabilities of its EVs. With a starting price of under $18,000, the Yuan Plus EV is an attractive option for consumers looking for an affordable and feature-rich electric vehicle. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/26/byds-new-yuan-plus-ev-adds-range-fast-charging-for-18000/)

## 02 Ganfeng Lithium Begins Production of 10 Ah Solid-State Batteries
Ganfeng Lithium, a Chinese lithium metal giant, has started producing the world's first 10 Ah solid-state batteries with an energy density of 500 Wh/kg. This milestone is a significant breakthrough in the development of solid-state batteries, which are expected to offer improved safety, energy density, and charging capabilities compared to traditional lithium-ion batteries. Ganfeng Lithium has supply deals with major OEMs, including Tesla, Volkswagen, and Hyundai, and its solid-state batteries are likely to be used in a range of applications, from electric vehicles to renewable energy systems. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/21/solid-state-ev-batteries-hit-another-major-milestone-china/)

## 03 Mercedes-AMG Develops Over-Engineered EV Battery with Silicon Anode
Mercedes-AMG engineers have developed a groundbreaking EV battery with a silicon anode, designed to contain heat and improve performance. The battery is described as "terrifyingly over-engineered" and features a range of innovative technologies, including a advanced cooling system and a high-performance silicon anode. The battery is expected to be used in the Mercedes-AMG GT, a high-performance electric vehicle that requires a powerful and reliable battery system. This development is significant for the battery industry, as it demonstrates the potential for innovative materials and designs to improve the performance and efficiency of EV batteries. [InsideEVs](https://insideevs.com/news/797013/silicon-anode-ev-battery-mercedes-amg-gt/)
