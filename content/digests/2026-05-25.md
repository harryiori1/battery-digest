## 01 CATL Launches China's First Standardized Light Truck Battery Swap Ecosystem
CATL has launched China's first standardized light truck battery swap ecosystem, a move that is expected to accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles in the country's logistics and transportation sectors. The ecosystem is designed to provide a convenient and efficient way for light truck owners to swap their batteries, reducing downtime and increasing overall vehicle productivity. With this development, CATL is poised to play a leading role in shaping China's electric vehicle industry, particularly in the commercial vehicle segment. The company's standardized battery swap ecosystem is expected to set a new benchmark for the industry, driving innovation and growth in the years to come. ## 02 Over 90% of Onvo L80 Buyers Opt for BaaS Battery Rental
In a significant trend, over 90% of initial Onvo L80 buyers have chosen the Battery-as-a-Service (BaaS) option, indicating a strong preference for flexible and cost-effective battery solutions. The BaaS model allows vehicle owners to rent their batteries, rather than purchasing them outright, providing a more affordable and manageable option for many consumers. This shift in consumer behavior is expected to have a profound impact on the electric vehicle industry, as manufacturers and suppliers adapt to changing market demands. The success of the BaaS model in the Onvo L80 launch is likely to encourage other companies to explore similar offerings, driving innovation and competition in the industry.
