## 01 BYD Surpasses Tesla as World’s Top Energy Storage Deployer
BYD has overtaken Tesla to become the world’s largest battery energy storage system (BESS) integrator, capturing 13% of the global market in 2025 compared to Tesla’s 10%, according to new data from Benchmark Mineral Intelligence. This shift marks the end of Tesla’s reign as the top energy storage deployer, a position it held in 2023 and 2024. Chinese manufacturers now dominate the rapidly growing energy storage market, with BYD leading the charge. This development is significant for the battery industry, as it highlights the growing importance of energy storage in the global transition to renewable energy. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/13/byd-surpasses-tesla-energy-storage-bess-benchmark-2025/)

## 02 BYD Eyes Stellantis EU Plant Takeover to Fuel Aggressive Overseas Expansion
BYD wants to take over underutilized plants in Europe from legacy automakers to fuel its aggressive overseas expansion. Executive vice president Stella Li confirmed that BYD is holding discussions not only with Stellantis but also with “other companies too.” This move is part of BYD’s strategy to increase its global production capacity and meet growing demand for its electric vehicles. The company has already seen significant success in China and is now looking to expand its presence in Europe and other markets. This development is important for the battery industry, as it highlights the growing demand for electric vehicles and the need for increased production capacity. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/13/byd-eyes-stellantis-eu-plant-ev-sales-surge-others-too/)

## 03 SEIA: AI Fuels Massive US Energy Storage Boom, Despite Trump Administration’s Attacks on Clean Energy
The US energy storage industry kicked off 2026 with its strongest Q1 on record, despite the Trump administration’s attacks on clean energy. According to the Solar Energy Industries Association (SEIA), AI is fueling a massive US energy storage boom, with installations reaching a record high in the first quarter of 2026. This development is significant for the battery industry, as it highlights the growing importance of energy storage in the US energy market. The use of AI in energy storage is also expected to drive further growth and innovation in the industry. [Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/05/20/seia-ai-is-fueling-a-massive-us-energy-storage-boom/)
