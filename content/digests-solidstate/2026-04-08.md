## 01 BYD's Chief Scientist Says Solid-State EV Batteries Hit a 'Critical Stage'
BYD's chief scientist, Lian Yubo, announced that solid-state EV batteries have entered a "critical stage" during a seminar this week, as [reported by Electrek](https://electrek.co/2026/04/08/byds-chief-scientist-solid-state-ev-batteries-critical-stage/). This development is significant for the commercialization of solid-state batteries, which promise to revolutionize the electric vehicle industry with their improved safety, energy density, and charging speeds. Although Lian Yubo did not provide specific details about the current status of BYD's solid-state battery technology, the fact that it has reached a critical stage suggests that the company is making significant progress in this area. As one of the leading electric vehicle manufacturers in China, BYD's advancements in solid-state battery technology could have a major impact on the industry as a whole.
